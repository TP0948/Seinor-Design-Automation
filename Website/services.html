Our Services


HomeAutomation is a housing automation solution designed for the landlord looking to outfit their property with the latest smart-home technologies. We provide a means to monitor and control your properties from anywhere in the world through our web-based service. Receive live updates, set schedules, and view long or short-term analytics, all from our robust and easy-to-use service.
